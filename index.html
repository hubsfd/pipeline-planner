What is a Qualified Prospect?

A qualified prospect is a contact or account that has been vetted against specific criteria and is genuinely worth pursuing — not just someone you've met or heard of. This is the key distinction between a suspect (anyone who could theoretically buy) and a prospect (someone who should buy, can buy, and is accessible to you).

At HUB, a qualified prospect meets all of the following:

Need: Has an identified insurance or risk management need you can address
Authority: You have access to the decision-maker or influencer
Ability to pay: The account is financially viable and within your target market
Timing: There is a realistic opportunity to engage within the next 12 months
Fit: The account aligns with your practice, expertise, or book of business goals
Receptive: They have agreed to a discovery conversation or expressed openness to one

When entering your numbers below, count only contacts who meet these criteria — not every name on a list or every card collected at an event.
